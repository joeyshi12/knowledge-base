Aggregate operators returns a single number

## Agregate Operators
- AVG(T.attr)
- MIN(T.attr)
- MAX(T.attr)
- SUM(T.attr)
- COUNT(T.attr)

AVG, SUM, COUNT have distinct counterparts which evaluates on distinct attributes:
- AVG(Distinct T.attr)
- SUM(Distinct T.attr)
- COUNT(Distinct T.attr)
